FAQ

Where can I find restructuring, debt, trade body and adviser news?

The latest news will appear on the homepage. Select deals, funds or people news from the menu at the top-right of the homepage. News content can also be filtered using the Filter by Topic panel on the right-hand side of the page to find news about topics such as deals or debt.

How do I view magazine content?

Each week well showcase fresh magazine content on the homepage. Use the Filter by Topic section on the right-hand side to view all historic magazine content

Where can I read Private Equity News latest features, analysis and comments?

See the menu at the top-right of the homepage to access all analysis and commentary, including in-depth features and long-form content from the magazine.

Will I continue to receive news bulletins?

Subscribers will continue to receive regular emails from Private Equity News, but in a new-look format. New subscribers can request a free trial on the homepage.

How do I customise news alerts?

Alerts can be activated/deactivated on the profile tab.
